# Email Marketing Swipe File — 50 Ready-to-Use Templates

### Copy. Paste. Customize. Send.

---

## How to Use This Swipe File

Every email below is ready to copy and paste. Replace anything in **[brackets]** with your specific details.

**Quick Tips:**
- Personalize the [Name] field — emails with first names get 26% higher open rates
- Keep subject lines under 50 characters for mobile
- Test different subject lines by sending to small segments first
- Send at optimal times: Tues-Thurs, 10am or 2pm local time for B2B; evenings & weekends for B2C
