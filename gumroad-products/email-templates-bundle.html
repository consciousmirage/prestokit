Subject Line
Here's your [Lead Magnet Name] — let's get started!
Email Body
Hey [First Name], Welcome! You just made a great decision. Here's your [Lead Magnet Name] as promised: [LINK] Here's how to get the most out of it: 1. [Quick action step #1] 2. [Quick action step #2] 3. [Quick action step #3] A little about me — I'm [Your Name], founder of [Business Name]. I help [target audience] [achieve specific result]. I started this because [one-sentence origin story]. Over the next few days, I'll share some of my best insights on [topic]. No fluff — just stuff that works. If you ever have questions, just hit reply. I read every message. Talk soon, [Your Name] P.S. If you want to skip ahead and see how I can help you [specific result], check out [Product/Service Link].
CTA: Download lead magnet + soft product mention

Email Body
Hey [First Name], Quick question — have you ever [describe common frustrating experience]? You're not alone. Most [target audience] make this one mistake: [Describe the mistake clearly — 2-3 sentences] The fix is actually simple: [Share a genuine, actionable tip that delivers an immediate result — 3-5 sentences] Try this today. It takes less than 10 minutes and the difference is noticeable. Tomorrow, I'll share [tease next email topic] — it's one of those things that sounds obvious but almost nobody does it right. Talk soon, [Your Name] P.S. Did you get a chance to check out [Lead Magnet Name]? If you have any questions about it, just reply to this email.
