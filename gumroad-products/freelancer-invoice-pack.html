How to Use

  • Print as PDF: Go to File → Print → Save as PDF. Each template prints on its own page.
  • Edit the text: Open this file in any text editor (VS Code, Notepad++, etc.) and replace the placeholder text with your real information.
  • Customize colors: Search for the color codes in the CSS (hex values like #4f46e5) and swap them for your brand colors.
  • Add your logo: Replace the company name text with an <img> tag pointing to your logo file.
Pro Tip To print just one template, use your browser's print dialog and select the specific page range for the template you want.

Placeholder Guide

  • [Your Company] — Replace with your business name
  • [Client Name] — Replace with your client's name/company
  • [INV-XXXX] — Replace with your invoice number
  • [Service Description] — Replace with actual line items
  • [Amount] — Replace with actual dollar amounts
  • [Date] — Replace with issue/due dates
  • [Payment Details] — Replace with your bank/PayPal/Venmo info
