Content Pillar Framework

The 5 types of content every business needs — with the right mix
40%
Educational / Value
Teach your audience something useful. Position yourself as the expert. This is the content that gets saved and shared.
Examples: How-to guides, tips, tutorials, frameworks, checklists, myth-busting, industry insights
20%
Engagement / Community
Start conversations. Ask questions. Create polls. The algorithm rewards comments and shares over likes.
Examples: Questions, polls, "this or that," AMAs, "what would you do?", hot takes, debate starters
15%
Behind the Scenes
Show the human side. Process shots, day-in-the-life, team moments, your workspace, raw and real content.
Examples: Origin story, work process, packing orders, team intros, workspace tour, "a day in my life"
15%
Promotional
Sell your stuff. But make it feel natural by leading with the transformation, not the product.
Examples: Product features, testimonials + CTA, before/after, limited offers, launch announcements
10%
Entertaining / Trending
Jump on trends, share memes, make people smile. This expands your reach to new audiences.
Examples: Trending audio, relatable humor, memes, pop culture references, light-hearted takes
